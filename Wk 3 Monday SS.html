Mughal Rule in India · 莫卧儿王朝统治印度

What Was the Mughal Empire? · 什么是莫卧儿帝国？

The Mughal Empire was one of the most powerful empires in world history. It ruled most of the Indian subcontinent from 1526 to 1857. The word "Mughal" comes from "Mongol" — the Mughals were descendants of the great Mongol and Turkic conquerors of Central Asia.

莫卧儿帝国是世界历史上最强大的帝国之一。它从1526年到1857年统治了印度次大陆的大部分地区。"莫卧儿"这个词来自"蒙古"——莫卧儿人是中亚伟大的蒙古和突厥征服者的后裔。

The Delhi Sultanate: Before the Mughals · 德里苏丹国：莫卧儿之前

Before the Mughals, India was ruled by the Delhi Sultanate (1206–1526). This was a series of Muslim kingdoms based in Delhi. The Sultanate brought Islam to northern India and built grand mosques and architecture. However, it was weakened by internal conflicts and was eventually defeated by Babur, the first Mughal emperor.

在莫卧儿人之前，印度由德里苏丹国（1206-1526年）统治。这是一系列以德里为基地的穆斯林王国。苏丹国将伊斯兰教带到印度北部，建造了宏伟的清真寺和建筑。然而，它因内部冲突而被削弱，最终被第一位莫卧儿皇帝巴布尔击败。

Key Contrast · 关键对比

Akbar allowed Hindus and Muslims to live peacefully together. Aurangzeb reversed this — he taxed Hindus and destroyed some Hindu temples. This religious conflict weakened the empire after Aurangzeb's death.

阿克巴允许印度教徒和穆斯林和平共处。奥朗则布颠覆了这一做法——他对印度教徒征税并摧毁了一些印度教寺庙。这种宗教冲突在奥朗则布去世后削弱了帝国。

Geographic Expansion · 地理扩张

The Mughal Empire began in Kabul and Lahore (modern Pakistan/Afghanistan). Over 200 years, it expanded south and east across the entire Indian subcontinent. Under Aurangzeb, it reached deep into the Deccan (southern India). At its peak, the Mughal Empire controlled about 150 million people — more than 25% of the world's population at the time.

莫卧儿帝国始于喀布尔和拉合尔（今巴基斯坦/阿富汗）。在200多年的时间里，它向南向东扩展到整个印度次大陆。在奥朗则布统治下，帝国深入德干高原（印度南部）。在鼎盛时期，莫卧儿帝国控制了约1.5亿人——占当时世界人口的25%以上。

Islamic Empires Compared · 伊斯兰帝国比较

Three Great Islamic Empires · 三大伊斯兰帝国

Between 1300 and 1700 CE, three powerful Islamic empires ruled large parts of the world: the Ottoman Empire, the Safavid Empire, and the Mughal Empire. Together they are called the "Gunpowder Empires" because all three used gunpowder weapons — cannons and muskets — to conquer territory and maintain power.

公元1300年至1700年间，三个强大的伊斯兰帝国统治着世界的大部分地区：奥斯曼帝国、萨法维帝国和莫卧儿帝国。它们合称"火药帝国"，因为这三个帝国都使用火药武器——大炮和火枪——来征服领土并维持权力。

Origins of Russia · 俄罗斯的起源

Where Did Russia Come From? · 俄罗斯从哪里来？

Russia is the largest country in the world today, but it began as a small group of Slavic people living near rivers in Eastern Europe. The first Russian state grew from a trading city called Novgorod and later the city of Kiev (modern-day Ukraine). These early states are called "Kievan Rus" — the ancestor of Russia, Ukraine, and Belarus.

俄罗斯是今天世界上最大的国家，但它起源于生活在东欧河流附近的一小群斯拉夫人。第一个俄罗斯国家从一个叫诺夫哥罗德的贸易城市发展而来，后来发展到基辅（今乌克兰）。这些早期国家被称为"基辅罗斯"——俄罗斯、乌克兰和白俄罗斯的祖先。

Key Stages of Russian Development · 俄罗斯发展的关键阶段

Stage 1: The Varangians (Vikings) · 阶段1：瓦良格人（维京人）

Around 862 CE, Slavic tribes invited a Viking leader named Rurik to rule over them. Rurik and his descendants (the Rurikid dynasty) built the first organized Russian state. The Vikings, called Varangians in Russia, were skilled traders and warriors who helped connect Russia to Byzantium (Constantinople) and the Middle East through river trade routes.

约公元862年，斯拉夫部落邀请一位名叫留里克的维京领袖来统治他们。留里克及其后裔（留里克王朝）建立了第一个有组织的俄罗斯国家。在俄罗斯被称为瓦良格人的维京人是技艺精湛的商人和战士，他们通过河流贸易路线将俄罗斯与拜占庭（君士坦丁堡）和中东连接起来。

Stage 2: Kievan Rus and Christianity · 阶段2：基辅罗斯与基督教

Kiev became the most important city. In 988 CE, Prince Vladimir of Kiev converted to Orthodox Christianity — the religion of the Byzantine Empire. This was one of the most important decisions in Russian history. It made Russia a Christian nation and connected it deeply to Byzantine culture, art, and law. The famous onion-dome churches of Russia come from this Byzantine influence.

基辅成为最重要的城市。公元988年，基辅王子弗拉基米尔皈依了东正教——拜占庭帝国的宗教。这是俄罗斯历史上最重要的决定之一。它使俄罗斯成为一个基督教国家，并将其与拜占庭文化、艺术和法律紧密联系在一起。俄罗斯著名的洋葱顶教堂就来自这种拜占庭影响。

Stage 3: The Mongol Invasion · 阶段3：蒙古入侵

In the 1230s–1240s, the Mongols under Batu Khan swept across Russia and destroyed most major cities, including Kiev. For about 240 years, Russian princes had to pay taxes and obey the Mongol Golden Horde. This period of Mongol rule, called the "Tatar Yoke," left a deep mark on Russia. Some historians argue it made Russian rulers more authoritarian and isolated Russia from Western European development.

13世纪30至40年代，拔都汗率领的蒙古人横扫俄罗斯，摧毁了包括基辅在内的大多数主要城市。约240年间，俄罗斯王公不得不向蒙古金帐汗国纳税并服从其统治。这段蒙古统治时期被称为"鞑靼枷锁"，在俄罗斯留下了深刻印记。一些历史学家认为，这使俄罗斯统治者更加专制，并使俄罗斯与西欧的发展相隔离。

Stage 4: Moscow Rises · 阶段4：莫斯科的崛起

While Kiev was destroyed, a small city called Moscow slowly grew in power. By the 1400s, Moscow's rulers — called Grand Princes — began uniting Russian lands and pushing back the Mongols. Ivan III ("Ivan the Great") in the 1460s–1505 refused to pay the Mongol tribute and declared Russia independent. He married a Byzantine princess and called Moscow the "Third Rome" — the new center of Christian civilization after Constantinople fell to the Ottomans in 1453.

基辅被摧毁时，一座叫莫斯科的小城市慢慢崛起。到15世纪，莫斯科的统治者——被称为大公——开始统一俄罗斯土地并抵抗蒙古人。伊凡三世（"伊凡大帝"）在1460至1505年间拒绝向蒙古人纳贡并宣布俄罗斯独立。他娶了一位拜占庭公主，并将莫斯科称为"第三罗马"——1453年君士坦丁堡落入奥斯曼人手中后基督教文明的新中心。

Big Idea · 核心概念

Russia's identity was shaped by three forces: Slavic roots, Orthodox Christianity, and Mongol rule. Each left a lasting mark on Russian government, culture, and relations with the outside world.

俄罗斯的身份认同由三种力量塑造：斯拉夫根源、东正教和蒙古统治。每一种都在俄罗斯的政府、文化和对外关系上留下了持久的印记。

Women in Rome & Han China · 罗马与汉朝的女性

Why Compare? · 为什么要比较？

Ancient Rome and Han China were the two greatest civilizations of their time — both existed roughly 200 BCE to 200 CE. Both had powerful governments, large armies, and complex social systems. But how did these civilizations treat women? By comparing them, we can learn how different cultures define gender roles and what factors shape women's lives.

古罗马和汉朝是同时代两个最伟大的文明——两者大约都存在于公元前200年至公元200年。两者都有强大的政府、庞大的军队和复杂的社会制度。但这些文明如何对待女性？通过比较，我们可以了解不同文化如何定义性别角色，以及哪些因素塑造了女性的生活。

Confucianism and Women in Han China · 儒学与汉朝女性

Han China (206 BCE – 220 CE) was deeply shaped by Confucianism — the philosophy of Confucius (551–479 BCE). Confucius taught a system of social order based on relationships. Women were expected to follow the "Three Obediences": obey their father before marriage, obey their husband after marriage, and obey their son in widowhood.

汉朝（公元前206年—公元220年）深受儒学影响——即孔子（公元前551—479年）的哲学。孔子教导了一种基于关系的社会秩序体系。女性被要求遵循"三从"：未嫁从父，既嫁从夫，夫死从子。

Key Confucian Teaching for Women: "Women's work" = weaving, cooking, raising children. Education was rare for women. Most women could not own property or hold government positions.

儒学对女性的主要教导："妇道"=纺织、烹饪、养育孩子。女性受教育的机会很少。大多数女性不能拥有财产或担任政府职位。

罗马共和国		 Some upper-class women gained influence through family connections. Could own property with male guardian. No voting.
一些上层阶级女性通过家庭关系获得影响力。可以在男性监护人的帮助下拥有财产。不能投票。

Important Comparison · 重要比较

In both Rome and Han China, most women had few legal rights. However, Roman women — especially in the Empire — had more freedom than women in Han China. They could run businesses, own property without a guardian, and even appear in public. Han women faced stronger restrictions based on Confucian ideals of obedience and hierarchy.

在罗马和汉朝，大多数女性的法律权利都很少。然而，罗马女性——尤其是帝国时期——比汉朝女性拥有更多自由。她们可以经营生意、无需监护人拥有财产，甚至可以在公共场合出现。汉朝女性面临着更严格的限制，这些限制基于儒家对服从和等级的理想。
