By the end of year 1 you will begin to see cross campus alignment on common pacing, assessments and instruction.

Quarter One
Sep – Nov 2026
Kotter 1–3 · Urgency · Coalition · Vision

  • System pacing documents distributed; all campuses aligned by end of September
  • Meeting with all new hires for the English department and revised onboarding experience
  • Spelling Bee relocated to fall; centralized purchasing and unified branding established
