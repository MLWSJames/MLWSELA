Week 1: Foundations of Civilization

Learning Focus

Human origins, agricultural revolution, classical empires, trade networks, and religious divisions

Homo sapiens
智人

Definition / 定义

Modern humans belong to the species called Homo sapiens.

现代人类属于称为智人的物种。

Characteristics / 特征

Large brain capacity, complex language, tool making, abstract thinking

大脑容量大, 复杂语言, 制造工具, 抽象思维

Examples / 例子

All humans alive today, Cro-Magnon people, early cave artists

当今所有人类, 克罗马农人, 早期洞穴艺术家

Non-Examples / 非例子

Neanderthals, Homo erectus, Australopithecus

尼安德特人, 直立人, 南方古猿

Neolithic Revolution
新石器时代革命

Definition / 定义

The Neolithic Revolution was when humans changed from hunting and gathering to settled farming.

新石器时代革命是人类从狩猎采集转变为定居农业的时期。

Characteristics / 特征

Agriculture and farming, permanent settlements, domestication of animals, food surplus

农业与耕作, 永久定居点, 动物驯化, 粮食剩余

Examples / 例子

Fertile Crescent farming, rice cultivation in China, first villages like Jericho

新月沃土农耕, 中国水稻种植, 杰里科等首批村庄

Non-Examples / 非例子

Paleolithic hunting bands, nomadic herding without farming, modern industrial farming

旧石器时代狩猎群体, 无农业的游牧放牧, 现代工业化农业

Hellenism
希腊化

Definition / 定义

Hellenism refers to the spread of Greek culture after Alexander the Great's conquests.

希腊化指亚历山大大帝征服后希腊文化的传播。

Characteristics / 特征

Greek language spread, Greek art and architecture, philosophy and science, blended cultures

希腊语传播, 希腊艺术与建筑, 哲学与科学, 文化融合

Examples / 例子

Alexandria Egypt, Greek-style temples in Asia, Koine Greek language, Hellenistic art

埃及亚历山大, 亚洲希腊式神庙, 通用希腊语, 希腊化艺术

Non-Examples / 非例子

Classical Athens before Alexander, pure Roman culture, pre-conquest Persian culture

古典雅典（亚历山大之前）, 纯罗马文化, 征服前波斯文化

Pax Romana
罗马和平

Definition / 定义

The Pax Romana was approximately 200 years of peace and prosperity in the Roman Empire.

罗马和平是罗马帝国约200年的和平与繁荣时期。

Characteristics / 特征

27 BCE to 180 CE, stable government, trade networks expanded, Roman law unified

公元前27年至公元180年, 稳定的政府, 贸易网络扩展, 罗马法统一

Examples / 例子

Augustus Caesar's rule, construction of roads, Roman aqueducts built, spread of Christianity

奥古斯都凯撒统治, 道路建设, 罗马水渠建造, 基督教传播

Non-Examples / 非例子

Civil wars of the late Republic, barbarian invasions later, any period of Roman civil war

共和国晚期内战, 蛮族入侵（后期）, 任何罗马内战时期

Silk Road
丝绸之路

Definition / 定义

The Silk Road was a network of trade routes connecting China, Central Asia, and Europe.

丝绸之路是连接中国、中亚和欧洲贸易路线的网络。

Characteristics / 特征

Overland and maritime routes, goods ideas diseases spread, no single road, active for centuries

陆路与海路, 商品思想疾病传播, 非单一道路, 活跃数世纪

Examples / 例子

Silk from China, spices from India, Buddhism spread to China, paper-making westward

中国丝绸, 印度香料, 佛教传入中国, 造纸术西传

Non-Examples / 非例子

Atlantic trade routes, modern shipping lanes, only silk was traded

大西洋贸易路线, 现代航运通道, 仅交易丝绸

Sunni-Shia Split
逊尼-什叶分裂

Definition / 定义

The Sunni-Shia split originated from a dispute over who should lead the Muslim community after the Prophet died.

逊尼-什叶分裂起源于关于先知去世后谁应领导穆斯林社区的争议。

Characteristics / 特征

Began 632 CE, Sunni elected leaders, Shia Ali's descendants, different religious practices

始于公元632年, 逊尼选举领袖, 什叶阿里后裔, 不同宗教习俗

Examples / 例子

Sunni majority worldwide, Shia in Iran Iraq, different hadith collections, Ashura commemorations

全球逊尼派多数, 伊朗伊拉克什叶派, 不同圣训集, 阿舒拉纪念

Non-Examples / 非例子

Christian denominations, Hindu castes, before Prophet Muhammad's death

基督教教派, 印度种姓, 先知穆罕默德去世前

Great Schism 1054
大分裂

Definition / 定义

The Great Schism (1054) divided Christianity into Roman Catholicism and Eastern Orthodox Christianity.

公元1054年的大分裂将基督教分为罗马天主教和东正教。

Characteristics / 特征

Authority dispute Pope vs Patriarch, language Latin vs Greek, Filioque controversy, mutual excommunication

权威争议教皇 vs 牧首, 语言拉丁语 vs 希腊语, 和子句争议, 互相绝罚

Examples / 例子

Roman Catholic Church, Eastern Orthodox Church, Byzantine Empire church, modern Greece Russia Orthodox

罗马天主教会, 东正教会, 拜占庭帝国教会, 现代希腊俄罗斯东正教

Non-Examples / 非例子

Protestant Reformation 1517, before 1054 unified church, non-Christian religions

宗教改革（1517年）, 1054年前统一教会, 非基督教宗教

Week 2: Islamic Golden Age, Mongol Empire and Americas

Learning Focus

Islamic caliphates, the Golden Age of science, Mongol stability, Aztec agriculture, and the devastating plague

Rashidun / Umayyad / Abbasid
正统/倭马亚/阿拔斯

Definition / 定义

The three early Islamic caliphates that expanded Muslim rule from Arabia across the Middle East, Africa, and Spain.

三个早期伊斯兰哈里发国，将穆斯林统治从阿拉伯扩展到中东、非洲和西班牙。

Characteristics / 特征

Rashidun 632-661 first 4 caliphs, Umayyad 661-750 Damascus capital, Abbasid 750-1258 Baghdad capital, rapid military expansion

正统632-661前四位哈里发, 倭马亚661-750大马士革首都, 阿拔斯750-1258巴格达首都, 快速军事扩张

Examples / 例子

Rashidun Abu Bakr Umar Uthman Ali, Umayyad Dome of the Rock built, Abbasid House of Wisdom, Al-Andalus Spain

正统阿布巴克尔欧麦尔奥斯曼阿里, 倭马亚圆顶清真寺建造, 阿拔斯智慧宫, 安达卢斯西班牙

Non-Examples / 非例子

Ottoman Empire later, Mughal Empire India, pre-Islamic Arabian tribes

奥斯曼帝国（后期）, 莫卧儿帝国（印度）, 伊斯兰前阿拉伯部落

Islamic Golden Age
伊斯兰黄金时代

Definition / 定义

The Islamic Golden Age was a period when Baghdad became a global center of science, math, medicine, and philosophy.

伊斯兰黄金时代是巴格达成为科学、数学、医学和哲学全球中心的时期。

Characteristics / 特征

8th to 13th centuries, translation movement, algebra astronomy optics, preservation of Greek texts

8至13世纪, 翻译运动, 代数天文学光学, 保存希腊文献

Examples / 例子

Al-Khwarizmi algebra, Ibn Sina Avicenna medicine, House of Wisdom library, advances in astronomy

花拉子米代数, 伊本西那阿维森纳医学, 智慧宫图书馆, 天文学进步

Non-Examples / 非例子

European Dark Ages decline, modern scientific method, pre-Islamic Arabian science

欧洲黑暗时代衰落, 现代科学方法, 伊斯兰前阿拉伯科学

Pax Mongolica
蒙古和平

Definition / 定义

The Pax Mongolica was a period of stability and safe trade across the Mongol Empire.

蒙古和平是蒙古帝国境内稳定与安全贸易的时期。

Characteristics / 特征

13th to 14th centuries, Silk Road protection, postal relay system, religious tolerance

13至14世纪, 丝绸之路保护, 驿站系统, 宗教宽容

Examples / 例子

Marco Polo's travels, spread of gunpowder, trade from China to Europe, Mongol postal stations

马可波罗旅行, 火药传播, 从中国到欧洲的贸易, 蒙古驿站

Non-Examples / 非例子

Mongol conquest wars, fragmented Europe trade, after Mongol Empire collapse

蒙古征服战争, 分裂的欧洲贸易, 蒙古帝国崩溃后

Chinampas
浮岛农业

Definition / 定义

Chinampas were the Aztec's floating garden agricultural system that supported the dense population of Tenochtitlan.

浮岛农业是阿兹特克人的浮动花园农业系统，支持了特诺奇蒂特兰密集的人口。

Characteristics / 特征

Rectangular plots on lake, mud and vegetation layers, willow trees for anchors, highly productive

湖上矩形地块, 泥土与植被层, 柳树作为锚定, 极高生产力

Examples / 例子

Lake Texcoco gardens, modern Xochimilco canals, 7-10 harvests per year, supported 200000 plus people

特斯科科湖花园, 现代霍奇米尔科运河, 每年7-10次收获, 支持20万+人口

Non-Examples / 非例子

European plow farming, terrace farming Andes, slash-and-burn agriculture

欧洲犁耕, 梯田农业（安第斯）, 刀耕火种农业

Black Death
黑死病

Definition / 定义

The Black Death was a bubonic plague that killed 1/3 to 1/2 of Europe's population in the 14th century.

黑死病是14世纪导致欧洲三分之一至一半人口死亡的腺鼠疫。

Characteristics / 特征

1347-1351 peak, Yersinia pestis bacteria, fleas on rats spread it, social and economic collapse

1347-1351年高峰, 鼠疫耶尔森菌, 老鼠身上的跳蚤传播, 社会与经济崩溃

Examples / 例子

75-200 million deaths globally, end of feudalism, labor shortages, flagellant movements

全球7500万至2亿人死亡, 封建制度终结, 劳动力短缺, 鞭笞派运动

Non-Examples / 非例子

Smallpox different disease, 1918 Spanish Flu, COVID-19 pandemic

天花（不同疾病）, 1918年西班牙流感, 新冠疫情

Mansa Musa
曼萨·穆萨

Definition / 定义

Mansa Musa was the Mali emperor whose 1324 pilgrimage to Mecca displayed extraordinary wealth and put Mali on the world map.

曼萨·穆萨是马里帝国的皇帝，他1324年的麦加朝圣展示了非凡的财富，使马里登上了世界地图。

Characteristics / 特征

Ruled c. 1312-1337, richest person in history, gave away gold in Cairo, built Timbuktu universities

统治约1312-1337年, 历史上最富有的人, 在开罗分发黄金, 建造廷巴克图大学

Examples / 例子

60000-person caravan, 12000 slaves with gold, Sankore University, Catalan Atlas depiction

6万人商队, 1.2万名携带黄金的奴隶, 桑科雷大学, 加泰罗尼亚地图集描绘

Non-Examples / 非例子

European kings of same era, Songhai Empire rulers, modern wealthy businessmen

同时代的欧洲国王, 桑海帝国统治者, 现代富商

Week 3: Enlightenment, Revolutions and Imperialism

Learning Focus

Natural rights philosophy, the French Revolution's dark turn, the Haitian slave revolution, Latin American independence, industrial imperialism, and China's Century of Humiliation

Natural Rights
天赋权利

Definition / 定义

Enlightenment idea: all people are born with natural rights (life, liberty, property) that no government can take away.

启蒙思想：所有人天生拥有天赋权利（生命、自由、财产），政府无权剥夺。

Characteristics / 特征

John Locke's philosophy, self-evident truths, universal to all humans, government protects them

约翰·洛克哲学, 不言而喻的真理, 全人类普遍拥有, 政府保护这些权利

Examples / 例子

US Declaration of Independence, French Declaration of Rights, UN Universal Declaration, life liberty pursuit of happiness

美国独立宣言, 法国人权宣言, 联合国世界人权宣言, 生命自由追求幸福

Non-Examples / 非例子

Rights granted by kings, divine right of monarchs, feudal obligations

国王授予的权利, 君权神授, 封建义务

Reign of Terror
恐怖统治

Definition / 定义

The Reign of Terror was when Robespierre executed thousands of enemies of the revolution during the French Revolution.

恐怖统治是罗伯斯庇尔在法国大革命期间处决数千名革命敌人的时期。

Characteristics / 特征

1793-1794, Committee of Public Safety, guillotine executions, 17000 plus officially executed

1793-1794年, 公共安全委员会, 断头台处决, 1.7万+官方处决

Examples / 例子

Maximilien Robespierre, Marie Antoinette executed, revolutionary tribunals, Liberty Equality Fraternity twisted

马克西米利安·罗伯斯庇尔, 玛丽·安托瓦内特被处决, 革命法庭, 自由平等博爱被扭曲

Non-Examples / 非例子

Initial 1789 revolution, Napoleon's rule, peaceful constitutional monarchy

1789年最初革命, 拿破仑统治, 和平君主立宪制

Haitian Revolution
海地革命

Definition / 定义

The Haitian Revolution (1791-1804) was the only successful slave revolution in history, creating the first Black republic.

海地革命（1791-1804年）是历史上唯一成功的奴隶革命，建立了第一个黑人共和国。

Characteristics / 特征

Toussaint Louverture leader, defeated French British Spanish, inspired by French Revolution, abolished slavery permanently

杜桑·卢维杜尔领导, 击败法国英国西班牙, 受法国大革命启发, 永久废除奴隶制

Examples / 例子

Saint-Domingue colony, Vodou ceremony start Bois Caiman, 1804 independence declaration, Napoleon's failed invasion

圣多明各殖民地, 巫毒仪式开端布瓦凯曼, 1804年独立宣言, 拿破仑失败入侵

Non-Examples / 非例子

American Revolution not slave-led, failed slave revolts before 1791, Latin American independence wars

美国革命（非奴隶领导）, 1791年前失败的奴隶起义, 拉丁美洲独立战争

El Libertador
解放者

Definition / 定义

Simon Bolivar earned the title 'El Libertador' by leading independence movements across five South American nations.

西蒙·玻利瓦尔通过领导五个南美国家的独立运动，赢得了"解放者"的称号。

Characteristics / 特征

1783-1830, Gran Colombia vision, military genius, inspired by Enlightenment

1783-1830年, 大哥伦比亚愿景, 军事天才, 受启蒙运动启发

Examples / 例子

Venezuela liberated 1813, Colombia Ecuador Peru Bolivia, Battle of Ayacucho, Bolivia named after him

委内瑞拉解放（1813年）, 哥伦比亚厄瓜多尔秘鲁玻利维亚, 阿亚库乔战役, 玻利维亚以他命名

Non-Examples / 非例子

George Washington North America, Jose de San Martin southern cone, European monarchs

乔治·华盛顿（北美）, 何塞·德·圣马丁（南部锥体）, 欧洲君主

19th-century Imperialism
19世纪帝国主义

Definition / 定义

Unlike earlier colonialism, 19th-century imperialism was driven by industrialization's need for raw materials and new markets.

与早期殖民主义不同，19世纪帝国主义是由工业化对原材料和新市场的需求驱动的。

Characteristics / 特征

New Imperialism 1870-1914, Scramble for Africa, technology gap guns ships, economic exploitation

新帝国主义（1870-1914）, 瓜分非洲, 技术差距（枪炮、舰船）, 经济剥削

Examples / 例子

British Raj in India, Belgian Congo, French Indochina, Berlin Conference 1884

英国在印度的统治, 比利时刚果, 法属印度支那, 柏林会议（1884年）

Non-Examples / 非例子

16th-17th century colonies, ancient Roman expansion, modern globalization

16-17世纪殖民地, 古罗马扩张, 现代全球化

Unequal Treaties
不平等条约

Definition / 定义

The Opium Wars forced China to sign unequal treaties that opened ports, ceded Hong Kong, and granted Western powers special privileges.

鸦片战争迫使中国签订不平等条约，开放港口，割让香港，并给予西方列强特殊特权。

Characteristics / 特征

Treaty of Nanking 1842, extraterritoriality, treaty ports, forced by military defeat

南京条约（1842年）, 领事裁判权, 通商口岸, 军事战败强迫签订

Examples / 例子

Treaty of Nanking Hong Kong, Treaty of Tientsin, Treaty of Shimonoseki, Boxer Protocol 1901

南京条约（香港）, 天津条约, 马关条约, 辛丑条约（1901年）

Non-Examples / 非例子

Equal diplomatic agreements, modern WTO negotiations, pre-Opium War trade

平等外交协议, 现代WTO谈判, 鸦片战争前贸易

Century of Humiliation
百年屈辱

Definition / 定义

China's Century of Humiliation began with the Opium Wars - a period when China faced foreign intervention, territorial concessions, and loss of sovereignty.

中国的百年屈辱始于鸦片战争——这一时期中国面临外国干预、领土割让和主权丧失。

Characteristics / 特征

c. 1839-1949, unequal treaties, foreign spheres of influence, nationalist trauma narrative

约1839-1949年, 不平等条约, 外国势力范围, 民族主义创伤叙事

Examples / 例子

First Opium War 1839-42, Treaty of Nanking, Sino-Japanese Wars, Boxer Rebellion, ended with PRC founding 1949

第一次鸦片战争（1839-42）, 南京条约, 中日战争, 义和团运动, 随中华人民共和国成立结束（1949年）

Non-Examples / 非例子

Ming Dynasty height, post-1949 PRC period, ancient Chinese empires

明朝鼎盛时期, 1949年后中华人民共和国时期, 古代中华帝国
