Tuesday: Word Choice, Connotation & Tone 星期二：词语选择、内涵与语气

Word choice (also called diction) is the specific selection of words an author uses. Every word an author picks is a decision — and that decision shapes the reader's experience.

词语选择（也叫 措辞）是作者使用特定词语的选择。作者选择的每个词都是一个决定——这个决定影响读者的体验。

Connotation is the emotional or cultural meaning a word carries beyond its dictionary definition (denotation). For example, "home" and "house" both describe a place to live, but "home" feels warm and personal, while "house" is neutral and structural.

内涵是词语超出字典释义（外延）之外所携带的情感或文化含义。例如，"家"和"房子"都描述居住的地方，但"家"给人温暖和亲切的感觉，而"房子"则中性且结构性。

Tone is the author's attitude toward the subject or audience, revealed through word choice. Tone can be hopeful, angry, sad, sarcastic, respectful, or many other attitudes. Changing even one word can shift an entire tone.

语气是作者通过词语选择传达的对主题或读者的态度。语气可以是充满希望的、愤怒的、悲伤的、讽刺的、恭敬的或其他多种态度。甚至改变一个词就可以改变整体语气。

🔑 Key Rules | 关键规则

  • Denotation = dictionary meaning  外延 = 字典含义
  • Connotation = emotional/cultural meaning  内涵 = 情感/文化含义
  • Positive connotation: words that feel good (e.g., "slim")  正面内涵：感觉好的词（如"苗条"）
  • Negative connotation: words that feel bad (e.g., "skinny")  负面内涵：感觉不好的词（如"瘦骨嶙峋"）
  • Tone is the author's attitude — identified through diction  语气是作者的态度——通过措辞识别
  • Same denotation ≠ same connotation  相同外延 ≠ 相同内涵

Wednesday: Elements of Poetry 星期三：诗歌元素

Poetry is a form of writing that uses language in a concentrated, musical way to express ideas and emotions. Unlike prose, poetry often uses line breaks, rhythm, and figurative language to create meaning.

诗歌是一种以集中、音乐性的方式使用语言来表达思想和情感的写作形式。与散文不同，诗歌常常使用换行、节奏和修辞语言来创造含义。

Key elements of poetry include:

诗歌的关键元素包括：

  • Speaker — the voice of the poem (not always the author). 说话者——诗歌的声音（不总是作者）。
  • Stanza — a group of lines, like a paragraph in poetry. 诗节——一组诗行，类似诗歌中的段落。
  • Rhyme & Rhyme Scheme — pattern of end sounds (labeled A, B, C…). 押韵和韵式——末尾音的模式（标记为A、B、C……）。
  • Rhythm — the beat or flow of a poem. 节奏——诗歌的节拍或流动。
  • Imagery — language that appeals to the senses. 意象——诉诸感官的语言。
  • Simile — a comparison using "like" or "as." 明喻——使用"像"或"如"的比较。
  • Metaphor — a direct comparison without "like" or "as." 暗喻——不用"像"或"如"的直接比较。
  • Personification — giving human traits to non-human things. 拟人——赋予非人事物人的特征。

🔑 Key Rules | 关键规则

  • Speaker ≠ author; identify the speaker's perspective 说话者 ≠ 作者；识别说话者的视角
  • A stanza is a grouped set of lines  诗节是一组诗行
  • Rhyme scheme: label each end sound A, B, C in order  韵式：按顺序标记每个末尾音
  • Imagery = any sensory detail (sight, sound, touch, taste, smell)  意象 = 任何感官细节（视觉、听觉、触觉、味觉、嗅觉）
  • Figurative language creates deeper meaning than literal words  修辞语言创造比字面意思更深的含义
