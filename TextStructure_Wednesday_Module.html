Concept Explanation 概念讲解

How Authors Organize Writing: Four Text Structures

When an author writes a text, they must decide how to arrange their ideas. This arrangement is called text structure. Choosing the right structure helps the reader follow the author's thinking.


Cause and Effect — the author explains what happened (cause) and what resulted (effect). Signal words: because, as a result, therefore, so, due to.

Problem and Solution — the author presents a difficulty and then explains how it can be fixed. Signal words: the problem is, one solution, this can be solved by.

Compare and Contrast — the author puts two things side by side. Signal words: however, on the other hand, similarly, in contrast, both.

Chronological Order — events are listed from first to last. Signal words: first, next, then, after that, finally.

文章结构是作者组织想法和信息的方式。
因果关系——解释原因和结果。比较与对比——展示两件事的异同。问题与解决方案——提出问题并解决。时间顺序——按先后顺序排列事件。

🔑 Key Rules / 重点规则

  • Signal words are clues to the structure. 信号词是结构的线索。
  • One text can use more than one structure. 一篇文章可以使用多种结构。
  • Knowing the structure helps you predict what comes next. 了解结构帮助你预测接下来的内容。
