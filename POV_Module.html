Point of View and the Reader · 叙述视角与读者

What is Point of View? · 什么是叙述视角？

Point of view (POV) is the position from which a story or piece of writing is told. It controls what information the reader receives and how the reader feels about the topic.

叙述视角（POV）是讲述故事或文章内容的角度。它决定读者获得哪些信息，以及读者对话题的感受。

Every author makes a choice about point of view. That choice is never accidental — it shapes your entire experience as a reader.

每位作者都会对叙述视角做出选择。这种选择从来不是偶然的——它决定了你作为读者的全部体验。

The Four Types of Narrator · 四种叙述者类型

Type · 类型Pronoun · 代词What the narrator knows · 叙述者知道什么Example · 例子
1st Person第一人称I, me, myOnly their own thoughts and feelings只了解自己的思想和感受"I felt afraid."
3rd Person Limited第三人称限制he, she, theyOne character's inner thoughts一个角色的内心想法"She felt afraid."
3rd Person Omniscient第三人称全知he, she, theyALL characters' thoughts and feelings所有角色的思想和感受"Both characters felt afraid."
Objective客观视角he, she, theyOnly external actions — no thoughts只有外部行动——没有内心想法"She walked away quickly."

Reliable vs. Unreliable Narrators · 可靠叙述者与不可靠叙述者

Reliable Narrator · 可靠叙述者 The narrator tells the truth. You can trust what they say. The story matches what really happened. 叙述者说的是实话。你可以相信他们说的话。故事与实际发生的事情相符。
Unreliable Narrator · 不可靠叙述者 The narrator's account cannot be fully trusted. They may be lying, mistaken, biased, or mentally unstable. 叙述者的叙述不能完全信任。他们可能在撒谎、犯错、有偏见或精神不稳定。
⚠️ Key Question · 关键问题

When you read, always ask: Why would this narrator see events this way? What are they NOT telling me?

阅读时要始终问自己：为什么这个叙述者会这样看待事件？他们没有告诉我什么？

Subjective vs. Objective Writing · 主观写作与客观写作

Subjective · 主观 Based on personal feelings, opinions, and experiences. The author's emotions influence the writing. 基于个人感受、观点和经历。作者的情感影响写作。 "The new park is a beautiful gift to the community."
Objective · 客观 Based on facts. The author does not express personal opinion. Both sides are presented equally. 基于事实。作者不表达个人观点。双方被平等呈现。 "The park opened in March and cost 2 million yuan."

How to Determine an Author's POV · 如何判断作者的观点

🔑 5-Step Method · 五步法

Use these steps every time you analyze a text for point of view.

每次分析文本中的叙述视角时都使用这些步骤。

  • 1
    Analyze word choice (diction) — Look for emotional, positive, or negative words. Words reveal attitude.分析词语选择（用词）——寻找情绪性、积极或消极的词语。词语揭示态度。
  • 2
    Notice what is included — What facts, examples, or stories does the author choose to include?注意包含的内容——作者选择包含哪些事实、例子或故事？
  • 3
    Notice what is excluded — What does the author leave out? Missing information can reveal bias.注意排除的内容——作者省略了什么？缺失的信息可以揭示偏见。
  • 4
    Check for bias — Does the author only show one side? Do they use loaded language? Do they ignore evidence?检查偏见——作者是否只展示一面？是否使用带有情绪色彩的语言？是否忽略证据？
  • 5
    Remember: it is their opinion — Author's POV is not the same as proven fact. Think critically.记住：这是他们的观点——作者的观点与经过证明的事实不同。要批判性思考。

Worked Example · 示例分析

"The city council's reckless decision will destroy our beloved neighborhood and erase years of hard work."

Word choice clues: reckless (negative judgment), destroy (extreme negative), beloved (emotional attachment), hard work (sympathy appeal). The author is clearly against the decision. This is subjective writing.

用词线索：鲁莽的（负面判断）、摧毁（极端负面）、心爱的（情感依附）、辛勤工作（同情诉求）。作者明显反对该决定。这是主观写作。

Analyzing Contrasting Points of View · 分析对比性叙述视角

What Characters See, Think, and Say · 角色所见、所想、所说

In a story, each character has their own point of view. A character's POV is shaped by their experiences, goals, fears, and personality. Two characters in the same scene can have completely different perspectives.

在故事中，每个角色都有自己的叙述视角。角色的观点由他们的经历、目标、恐惧和个性决定。同一场景中的两个角色可以有完全不同的看法。

Three Lenses for Character POV · 分析角色视角的三个维度

👁 What They SEE他们所见 The events and details they observe. A character only sees what is in front of them. 他们观察到的事件和细节。角色只能看到眼前的事物。
💭 What They THINK他们所想 Their inner thoughts, opinions, and judgments. These are private unless the narrator reveals them. 他们内心的想法、观点和判断。除非叙述者揭示，否则这些是私密的。
🗣 What They SAY他们所说 Their dialogue. Characters may say things that do NOT match what they think. 他们的对话。角色可能说的话与他们想的不一致。
💡 Key Insight · 关键认识

What a character SAYS and what they THINK are often different. This gap is called dramatic irony — the reader knows more than the character (or another character) knows.

角色所说的和他们所想的往往不同。这个差距叫做戏剧性反讽——读者知道的比角色（或另一个角色）知道的更多。

Character POV vs. The Story's Message · 角色视角与故事主旨

A character's point of view is limited and personal. The story's overall message (theme or central idea) is bigger — it is what the author wants ALL readers to understand.

角色的观点是有限且个人的。故事的整体主旨（主题或中心思想）更广泛——它是作者希望所有读者理解的内容。

Character POV · 角色视角Story's Message · 故事主旨
Personal · 个人的Universal · 普遍的
Limited by their experience · 受经历限制Goes beyond any one character · 超越任何单一角色
May be wrong or biased · 可能是错误的或有偏见的Reflects the author's purpose · 反映作者意图
Found in: dialogue, thoughts, actions · 在对话、想法、行动中体现Found in: patterns, endings, contrasts · 在模式、结局、对比中体现

How to Compare Contrasting POVs · 如何比较对比性观点

  • 1
    Identify each character's goal or motivation — What does each character want? Why do they want it?确定每个角色的目标或动机——每个角色想要什么？为什么？
  • 2
    Find where they agree and disagree — What do they see the same way? Where does the conflict come from?找出他们同意和不同意的地方——他们对什么看法相同？冲突来自哪里？
  • 3
    Ask: who has more information? — Does one character know something the other doesn't? How does this create tension?问：谁有更多信息？一个角色知道另一个角色不知道的事吗？这如何制造紧张感？
  • 4
    Compare to the author's message — Does the story validate one POV? Or does it show that both characters are partly right and partly wrong?与作者主旨比较——故事是否支持某一观点？还是显示两个角色各有对错？

Worked Example: Two Characters, Same Scene · 示例：同一场景，两个角色

A factory closes.
Worker's POV: "This is a disaster. My family will have no income. The owners are greedy."
Owner's POV: "This was the only choice. We had no money left. I tried to save the company."

Both POVs are real — both are limited. The story's message might be: economic hardship damages everyone, and there are no simple villains.

工厂关闭了。工人的观点："这是灾难。我的家庭将没有收入。老板们太贪婪了。"老板的观点："这是唯一的选择。我们没有钱了。我努力挽救公司。"两种观点都是真实的——都是有限的。故事的主旨可能是：经济困难伤害所有人，没有简单的坏人。
