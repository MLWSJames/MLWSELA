History vs. Prehistory · 历史与史前

History uses written records. Prehistory has no written records — we learn from tools, bones, and cave paintings.

历史使用文字记录。史前没有文字记录——我们通过工具、骨骼和洞穴壁画来了解。

The Neolithic Revolution · 新石器时代革命

A major turning point: humans changed from nomadic hunter-gathering to settled farming. This led to permanent settlements, population growth, and eventually civilization.

重大转折点：人类从游牧狩猎采集转变为定居农耕。这导致了永久定居点、人口增长，最终产生了文明。

📌 Key for Study Guide / 学习指南要点 The Neolithic Revolution changed humans from nomadic hunter-gatherers → settled farmers. Homo sapiens first evolved in Africa. 新石器时代革命使人类从游牧狩猎采集者变为定居农民。智人最早在非洲进化。

Mesopotamia — "Land Between the Rivers" · 两河之间的土地

Developed between the Tigris and Euphrates rivers. The Epic of Gilgamesh is one of the world's earliest surviving pieces of literature.

在底格里斯河和幼发拉底河之间发展。《吉尔伽美什史诗》是世界上最早留存的文学作品之一。

Battle of Kadesh & Peace Treaty · 卡迭石战役与和约

Fought between Egypt and the Hittites (c. 1274 BCE). The resulting peace treaty is considered one of the earliest surviving peace treaties in recorded history.

发生在埃及与赫梯之间（约公元前1274年）。由此产生的和约被认为是历史上最早留存的和约之一。

Connecting Pattern — River Civilizations · 联系模式——河流文明

The most significant pattern connecting Mesopotamia, Egypt, Indus Valley, and China: all developed near major rivers that supported agriculture and population growth.

连接美索不达米亚、埃及、印度河流域和中国的最重要模式：所有文明都在支持农业和人口增长的大河附近发展。

🌏 Americas · 美洲 Pre-contact civilizations (Olmec, Maya, Mississippian) developed complex societies independently. They were NOT simple or primitive. 接触前的文明（奥尔梅克、玛雅、密西西比）独立发展了复杂社会，并非简单或原始。

Greek Philosophers · 希腊哲学家

Socrates, Plato, and Aristotle developed philosophical methods of reasoning that shaped Western thought for thousands of years.

苏格拉底、柏拉图和亚里士多德发展了影响西方思想数千年的哲学推理方法。

After Alexander's Death · 亚历山大死后

His empire was divided among his generals (the Diadochi / 继业者). It was NOT peacefully inherited or conquered by Rome immediately.

他的帝国被他的将领们（继业者）瓜分。并非和平继承，也不是立即被罗马征服。

Fall of Rome — Internal Causes · 罗马衰落——内部原因

Political instability, corruption, economic troubles, and military overextension weakened Rome from within. External invasions delivered the final blow.

政治不稳定、腐败、经济困难和军事过度扩张从内部削弱了罗马。外部入侵给予了最后一击。

⚖️ Patricians vs. Plebeians · 贵族与平民 The two main social classes of Rome: wealthy elite (patricians) and the common people (plebeians). Not military ranks or types of priests. 罗马两大主要社会阶层：富裕精英（贵族）和普通民众（平民）。不是军衔或祭司类型。

Medieval Feudalism · 中世纪封建主义

A hierarchy of lords and vassals exchanging land (fiefs) for military service and loyalty. The Catholic Church was the most powerful institution in medieval society.

领主和封臣之间用土地（采邑）换取军事服务和忠诚的等级制度。天主教会是中世纪社会最强大的机构。

Columbian Exchange · 哥伦布大交换

Transfer of plants, animals, diseases, and ideas between the Old World and the Americas after 1492. Most devastating consequence for Indigenous peoples: European diseases killed millions who had no immunity.

1492年后旧世界与美洲之间动植物、疾病和思想的交流。对原住民最具破坏性的后果：欧洲疾病杀死了数百万没有免疫力的人。

📌 3 G's of Exploration / 探索的三个G God (religious mission) + Glory (national prestige) + Gold (economic profit) = why Europe explored. 上帝（宗教使命）+荣耀（国家声望）+黄金（经济利益）=欧洲探索的原因。

政治不稳定、经济压力、军事过度扩张、外部入侵
