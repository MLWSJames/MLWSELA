Integrity mode (block copy, log tab-switches) / 诚信模式
Original classroom tool, built on the public-domain Rasch adaptive model. The 77-item pool spans number sense through early high-school algebra and geometry. No proprietary questions, scales, or norms from any commercial product. The Math Power Score (MPS) is calibrated to this item pool — useful for placement, grouping, and growth across re-tests, not a nationally-normed grade.
DIRECTIONS · 说明

How this works / 测试说明

1. Solve each problem and choose the best answer.
解答每道题，选择最佳答案。

2. The test gets harder or easier based on your answers. Some problems will feel hard — that's normal.
题目会根据你的回答变难或变易。有些题感觉难是正常的。

3. You cannot go back. You may use scratch paper, but no calculator unless your teacher allows it.
不能返回上一题。可以用草稿纸；除非老师允许，否则不要用计算器。

4. There is no time limit. Do your best!
没有时间限制，尽力而为！

Integrity mode is ON. Please stay on this tab until you finish. 诚信模式已开启，请勿切换页面，直到完成测试。
